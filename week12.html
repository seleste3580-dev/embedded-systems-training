Week 12 - Capstone Project Development & Final Presentations

Objectives

The main objectives of the final week of the embedded systems training program were to:

  • Develop, assemble, and test the final capstone project.
  • Integrate hardware components with the software logic written in previous weeks.
  • Troubleshoot and debug any integration issues.
  • Prepare and deliver individual project presentations to mark the conclusion of the training program.

Overview

Key outcomes and activities from Week 12:

  • Actively coded, wired, and prototyped the capstone project, bringing the theoretical design from Week 11 into reality.
  • Refined the software, ensuring stable communication between the ESP32 and all connected peripherals.
  • Prepared a comprehensive presentation detailing the problem statement, methodology, challenges faced, and the final working solution.
  • Participated in the final presentation sessions, showcasing individual projects to peers and instructors.
  • Successfully completed the embedded systems training program!

This week was a tremendous wrap-up to the entire program, demonstrating all the skills acquired—from basic electronics to advanced microcontroller programming and IoT development.
