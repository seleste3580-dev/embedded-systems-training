AquaMonitor: Smart Water Management System

"Ensuring Water Sustainability Through Intelligent Monitoring"

Project Overview

AquaMonitor is an advanced embedded system designed to revolutionize water management in residential and industrial environments. By leveraging IoT technology and precision sensors, AquaMonitor provides real-time data on water consumption, quality, and storage levels.

The project aims to address the global challenge of water scarcity by providing users with actionable insights to minimize waste and ensure the safety of their water supply.

Key Features

  • Real-time Level Monitoring: Uses ultrasonic sensors to track water levels in tanks with high precision.
  • Water Quality Analysis: Integrates pH and turbidity sensors to ensure the water is safe for consumption.
  • Leak Detection: Sophisticated algorithms detect unusual flow patterns and alert users to potential leaks.
  • Cloud Integration: Powered by the ESP32, data is synced to a dashboard for remote monitoring and analytics.
  • Automated Controls: Smart valve control to prevent overflows and manage distribution efficiently.
